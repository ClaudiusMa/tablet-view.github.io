Jevelry Design

Pictographs evolved from pictorial characters, unlike phonetic text pictographs which are ideographic.

I reversed the ideographic text with the original graphic, and then reorganized the graphic with the current intention, thus completing the fusion of ancient Chinese characters with modern design.

This is very obvious in the design of Beijing. Urban layouts are not the same in every country, and since ancient times Beijing has been a "井" layout. “Road” becomes an element in Beijing, and I first made "北京(Beijing)" back to a graphic and then integrated the road element into it. This one figure and this new figure form a new ideogram. Similarly, I incorporated chopsticks into the "家(family)” and red into “庆(celebration)”.
